# Traffic Dataset Catalog

本目录为第 4-8 章实践准备，优先选择公开可访问、适合教学复现、适合编程和工程实践的数据源。

正式使用前请再次核对各数据集的许可证、引用要求、账号申请要求和再分发规则。

## 第 4 章 交通面板数据分析

推荐主数据集：

- NYC Motor Vehicle Collisions Crashes: https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Crashes/h9gi-nx95

可选扩展：

- FHWA Highway Statistics Series: https://www.fhwa.dot.gov/policyinformation/statistics.cfm
- NHTSA FARS: https://www.nhtsa.gov/research-data/fatality-analysis-reporting-system-fars
- NYC Open Data Traffic Volume Counts: https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Transportation/Traffic-Volume-Counts/btm5-ppia
- National Transit Database: https://www.transit.dot.gov/ntd

## 第 5 章 交通位置数据分析

推荐主数据集：

- Microsoft GeoLife GPS Trajectories: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/geolife-gps-trajectory-dataset-user-guide/

可选扩展：

- T-Drive Taxi Trajectories: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/t-drive-trajectory-data-sample/
- NYC TLC Trip Record Data: https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/about/tlc-trip-record-data.page
- Capital Bikeshare System Data: https://capitalbikeshare.com/system-data

## 第 6 章 交通时序数据分析

推荐主数据集：

- Caltrans PeMS: https://pems.dot.ca.gov/

可选扩展：

- METR-LA: https://github.com/liyaguang/DCRNN
- PEMS-BAY: https://github.com/liyaguang/DCRNN
- Chicago Transit Authority Ridership: https://data.cityofchicago.org/Transportation/CTA-Ridership-Daily-Boarding-Totals/6iiy-9s97

## 第 7 章 交通时空数据分析

推荐主数据集：

- PeMSD4 / PeMSD8: https://github.com/guoshnBJTU/ASTGCN-r-pytorch

可选扩展：

- METR-LA sensor graph: https://github.com/liyaguang/DCRNN
- PEMS-BAY sensor graph: https://github.com/liyaguang/DCRNN

## 第 8 章 交通影像数据分析

推荐主数据集：

- BDD100K: https://www.bdd100k.com/

可选扩展：

- UA-DETRAC: https://detrac-db.rit.albany.edu/
- AI City Challenge: https://www.aicitychallenge.org/
- KITTI Vision Benchmark: https://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/
